    • Love

    When you bring a dog to your home, it becomes a part of your family. The love of a dog is unconditional. The bond between a human and a dog is uncomparable. A dog can teach a human many things about love. A dog will be loyal and protective if he knows that he is loved.


    Food

    Good nutrition and a balanced diet are important in keeping your dog healthy. What you feed your dog will affect it's health. Water should always be given to a dog to prevent dehydration. There are many different types of dog foods. Depending on the size of your dog and how active it is will determine how much you will feed the dog.


    Excercise

    Excercise for dogs is just as important as it is for humans. There are many different ways you can excercise your dog. Playing catch, fetch, running or just going for a long walk. Excercise will benefit your dog. The amount of excercise a dog needs is based on it's age, it's size, the breed, and overall health. Certain dogs will need more excercise than others.


    Shots

    When it comes to your dog's health it is important to make sure they receive the proper vaccinations. A puppy needs core vaccines at 6, 12, and 16 weeks old. A puppy will also need a rabies shot. Once they get older they need to have a rabies shot every three years.
